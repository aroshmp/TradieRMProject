# User Stories

Based on Business Requirements (BR1–BR10)

---

## BR1 – CRUD for Customers, Technicians, Jobs, and Parts

**US1.1 – Manage Customers**
> As an **administrator**, I want to create, view, update, and delete customer records, so that I can maintain accurate and up-to-date customer information in the system.

**US1.2 – Manage Technicians**
> As an **administrator**, I want to create, view, update, and delete technician profiles, so that I can keep track of available technicians and their details.

**US1.3 – Manage Jobs**
> As an **administrator**, I want to create, view, update, and delete jobs, so that I can track all service requests and their current status.

**US1.4 – Manage Job Information and Parts**
> As an **administrator**, I want to add, view, update, and remove parts and information associated with a job, so that I can maintain a complete and accurate record of what each job entails.

---

## BR2 – API / Webhook Integration with External Website

**US2.1 – Submit Job Request via Website**
> As a **client**, I want to fill out a contact form on the company's website, so that my job request is automatically submitted into the system without needing to call or email directly.

**US2.2 – Receive Job Requests via API/Webhook**
> As an **administrator**, I want the system to receive job requests from an external website via an API or webhook, so that new client requests are automatically captured and ready for processing.

---

## BR3 – Automatic Confirmation Response

**US3.1 – Automatic Acknowledgement to Client**
> As a **client**, I want to automatically receive a confirmation message after submitting a job request, so that I know the company has received my request and is aware of my needs.

---

## BR4 – AI-Generated or Curated Response Suggestion

**US4.1 – Response Suggestion for Administrator**
> As an **administrator**, I want the system to suggest a curated or AI-generated response to a client's request, so that I can respond quickly and reduce the time spent on routine communications.

**US4.2 – Response Suggestion for Technician**
> As a **technician**, I want to be provided with a suggested response when replying to a client query, so that I can communicate professionally and efficiently without drafting messages from scratch.

---

## BR5 – Human Approval Before Sending AI Content

**US5.1 – Administrator Approval of AI Response**
> As an **administrator**, I want to review and approve any AI-generated response before it is sent to a client, so that I can ensure the content is accurate, appropriate, and free from security or privacy risks.

**US5.2 – Technician Approval of AI Response**
> As a **technician**, I want to review and approve any AI-generated message before it is sent to a client, so that I remain in control of communications and can prevent unintended or incorrect information from being shared.

---

## BR6 – Technician Timetable with Travel Time

**US6.1 – View Technician Schedule**
> As an **administrator**, I want to view a technician's timetable of assigned jobs, so that I can see their availability and avoid scheduling conflicts.

**US6.2 – Auto-Adjust for Travel Time**
> As an **administrator**, I want the system to automatically account for travel time between jobs when scheduling a technician, so that technicians are not overbooked and can arrive on time for each appointment.

**US6.3 – Technician Views Own Schedule**
> As a **technician**, I want to view my daily schedule including travel time between jobs, so that I can plan my day effectively and arrive at each job on time.

---

## BR7 – Job Booking by Administrators or Clients

**US7.1 – Administrator Books a Job**
> As an **administrator**, I want to book a date and time for a job to be completed, so that I can schedule technicians based on conversations with clients over the phone or in person.

**US7.2 – Client Books a Job**
> As a **client**, I want to select a preferred date and time for a technician to complete my job, so that the appointment fits around my schedule.

---

## BR8 – Personal Calendar Synchronisation

**US8.1 – Sync with Google Calendar**
> As a **technician**, I want to synchronise my job schedule with my Google Calendar, so that I can view my work appointments alongside my personal commitments in one place.

**US8.2 – Sync with Outlook Calendar**
> As a **technician**, I want to synchronise my job schedule with my Outlook Calendar, so that I can manage my time effectively without switching between multiple applications.

---

## BR9 – Desktop and Mobile Web Design

**US9.1 – Desktop Interface for Administrators**
> As an **administrator**, I want to access the system through a desktop web browser, so that I can efficiently manage customers, jobs, and technicians from my workstation.

**US9.2 – Mobile Interface for Technicians**
> As a **technician**, I want to access the system on my mobile device via a web browser, so that I can view job details and add notes while I am on-site completing a job.

---

## BR10 – Invoice Generation

**US10.1 – Generate Job Invoice**
> As an **administrator**, I want the system to automatically generate an invoice once a job is completed, detailing the labour costs and parts used, so that the client receives an accurate and professional billing document.

**US10.2 – Client Receives Invoice**
> As a **client**, I want to receive a detailed invoice after my job is completed, including labour, parts, total costs, and payment instructions, so that I know exactly what I am being charged for and how to pay.

---

*Total User Stories: 18*
